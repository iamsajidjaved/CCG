MESSAGE

Cadet College Gujrat aims to provide quality education to its cadets with special focus on creativity, physical training, co-curricular and extra co-curricular activities, personal grooming, character building and excellent communication skills. Our endeavor is to train the cadets in a healthy and conducive environment and develope their wholesome personality with strong character, academic excellence, physical robustness and intellectual superiority. Our mission is to groom them as useful members of the society who are well aware of their religious, social and national obligations.

I wish and pray that the prestigious institution must set higher standards in imparting quality education in order to produce visionary and patriotic citizens who could successfully play their role in all fields of life.
